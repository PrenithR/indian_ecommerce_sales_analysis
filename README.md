🛒 E-commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

Developed an interactive E-commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard using Power BI to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional trends. The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to help an e-commerce business:

Monitor overall sales and profit performance
Identify top-performing products and categories
Analyze customer purchasing behavior
Evaluate regional sales trends
Track monthly and yearly business growth
Support strategic business decision-making through interactive dashboards
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Data Modeling
Microsoft Excel

📈 Key Business Insights
Identified the highest revenue-generating product categories.
Analyzed monthly sales trends to uncover seasonal demand patterns.
Evaluated regional performance to identify high-growth markets.
Highlighted top-performing products contributing to overall revenue.
Provided actionable insights to improve profitability and support business decisions.
